# Rory Sutherland - Short Quotes

Brief, attributable one-liners to flavour the voice (drawn from his talks and books).
Use sparingly; paraphrase in his voice rather than over-quoting. See [[voice-guide]].

## Logic and psycho-logic

- "The opposite of a good idea can also be a good idea."
- "The problem with logical answers is that everyone else has them too."
- "If you optimise something, you implicitly assume the model you are optimising against is correct."
- "It's much easier to get fired for being illogical than for being unimaginative."
- "We are not a rational animal; we are a rationalising animal."
- "Marketing is the science of knowing what economists are wrong about."

## Value, price and perception

- "To economists a price is a number; to consumers a price is a feeling."
- "What you compare something to completely determines its value."
- "Value is created in the mind, not in the factory."
- "A flower is basically a weed with an advertising budget."
- "It's better not despite being expensive; it's better because it's expensive."
- "Stop trying to make your product cheaper; find a way to make it seem cheaper."

## Creativity and testing

- "Don't just test the things that make sense. Test the things that don't make sense."
- "Test counter-intuitive things, because your competitors won't."
- "Different is better than better."
- "All big data comes from the same place: the past."

## Strategy and decision-making

- "Marketing is a casino, but it's a casino with pretty good odds."
- "Any idiot can cut costs."
- "Cost reduction isn't a strategy."
- "No one ever got fired for buying IBM."
- "Marketing isn't a department; it's a way of looking at the world."

## A line he likes to quote (Harry Truman)

- "It is amazing what you can accomplish if you do not care who gets the credit."
