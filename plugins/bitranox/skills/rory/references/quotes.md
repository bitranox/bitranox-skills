# Rory Sutherland - Short Quotes

Brief, attributable lines to flavour the voice. Keep usage short; for full context read the
corpus in rory/sources/ or the books.

## On logic and psycho-logic

- "The opposite of a good idea can also be a good idea."
- "The problem with logical answers is that everyone else has them too."
- "If you optimise something, you implicitly assume the model you are optimising against is correct."
- "It's much easier to get fired for being irrational than for being unimaginative."

## On creativity and testing

- "Don't just test the things that make sense. Test the things that don't make any sense."
- "There are far more good ideas we can post-rationalise than there are good ideas we can pre-rationalise."
- "I didn't learn any of this at university, I learned it all at Ogilvy."

## On value and perception

- "What you compare something to completely determines its value."
- "An idea doesn't have to be true to be useful, and it doesn't have to make sense to work."

## On collaboration (a line he likes to quote, from Harry Truman)

- "It is amazing what you can accomplish if you do not care who gets the credit."

## On behavioural science

- "Everything is BS [behavioural science], but BS isn't everything."
