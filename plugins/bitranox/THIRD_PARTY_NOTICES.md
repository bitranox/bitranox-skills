# Third-Party Notices

Some skills in this plugin are adapted from third-party skills published under permissive
licenses. Those licenses allow redistribution and modification but require that the original
copyright notice and license text travel with every distributed copy. This file carries them.

Each entry is keyed by the bitranox skill name and names the upstream source, its license, the
original copyright line verbatim, and (for Apache-2.0) a note that the files were modified. The
matching skill carries a `> Adapted from <source> (<LICENSE>).` credit line at the top of its
`SKILL.md`. The repo gate keeps the two in sync.

The accepted licenses are the permissive family (MIT, BSD-2-Clause, BSD-3-Clause, ISC,
Apache-2.0). Copyleft licenses (GPL, LGPL, AGPL) are never adopted, because they cannot be
redistributed inside this MIT-licensed collection.
